Accenture - Liquid Studio

From March 2022 to February 2023, I served as an Advanced App Engineering Analyst at Accenture's Liquid Studio, focusing on Front End Engineering for various high-profile clients.

One of my projects was developing a FullStack React Native application within a tight one-month deadline, serving as a Proof of Concept for Gasunie, a Dutch energy company. This app was designed to showcase innovative solutions in the energy sector, emphasizing rapid development and deployment capabilities.

I also played a role in the creation of a Blockchain-based mobile application used for asset tracking. This project involved intricate front-end development and integration with blockchain technology, providing transparent and secure tracking solutions for assets within Liquid Studio.

Additionally, I was part of a team working on a large-scale application aimed at digitally connecting energy and water companies across Germany. My involvement included developing robust front-end functionalities that facilitated seamless interactions between different stakeholders in the utility sector.

Furthermore, I undertook the role of a DevOps engineer for a frontend application at Rabobank. In this role, I maintained an internal website used for keeping track of bought Chamber of Commerce records and buying new ones.
